Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

Do you accept my insurance? ▾

Yes! We accept all major insurance plans including Medicare, Medicaid, Horizon, United Healthcare, and many more. Call us if you're unsure — we likely cover yours.

How do I get my prescription delivered? ▾

Simply call us or ask at the counter. We offer free home delivery within our service area. Just let us know your address and we'll take care of the rest.

How long does it take to fill a prescription? ▾

Most prescriptions are filled the same day — often within the hour. For new prescriptions, we recommend calling ahead or dropping off early to ensure the quickest turnaround.

Can I transfer my prescription from another pharmacy? ▾

Absolutely. Just bring your old prescription bottle or give us the pharmacy's number and we'll handle the transfer for you — no hassle at all.

Do you have a pharmacist I can speak with? ▾

Yes! Our licensed pharmacists are always available to answer your questions, review your medications, and provide counseling. We're here to help — don't hesitate to ask.
