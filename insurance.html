Insurance Questions

Common questions about insurance and billing at TriPharm.

Does it cost anything to transfer my prescription? ▾

No — transferring your prescription to TriPharm is completely free. There are no fees or charges associated with the transfer process. We handle it all at no cost to you.

Will my copay be different at TriPharm? ▾

Your copay is determined by your insurance plan, not by the pharmacy. In most cases, your cost will be the same as any other in-network pharmacy. We can check your specific plan before you transfer.

What if I don't have insurance? ▾

We serve patients with and without insurance. We accept discount cards like GoodRx and can discuss cash pricing options. We'll always work to find the most affordable option for you.

Can I use GoodRx or other discount cards? ▾

Yes! We accept GoodRx and most pharmacy discount cards. Just present the card at the time of pickup. This can sometimes save you significant money even if you have insurance.

How long does a transfer take? ▾

Most transfers are completed within a few hours. In many cases, we can have your prescription filled the same day you request the transfer. We'll contact you as soon as it's ready.

It's simple, fast, and free. We'd love to be your pharmacy.
